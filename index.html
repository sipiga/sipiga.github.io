About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher now working at University of Hamburg. Before that I was working with Andrew Treglown at University of Birmingham. I did my Ph.D. in Hamburg under the supervision of Mathias Schacht. Previously, back in Chile, I wrote my master's thesis at Universidad de Chile under the supervision of Maya Stein.

I'm interested in extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, especially in problems for hypergraphs and finite sets.
